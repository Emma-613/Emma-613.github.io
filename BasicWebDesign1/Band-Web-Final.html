About the Band

Golden Velvet is a band that debuted on June 16th, 2026. They've seen steady success following their debut album 'For Love and Roses' due to it's consistently unique production and deep lyrics across all 12 included songs. The band broke out in August of 2027 with their hit single 'Damage' that hit #1 for 6 weeks on the Billboard Hot 100.
