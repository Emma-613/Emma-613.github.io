GOLDEN VELEVET

About the Band

Golden Velvet is a global band that debuted on June 16th, 2026. They've seen steady success following their debut album 'For Love and Roses' due to it's consistently unique production and deep lyrics across all 12 included songs. The band broke out in August of 2027 with their hit single 'Damage' that hit #1 for 6 weeks on the Billboard Hot 100.

The Origin

Although Golden Velvet was officially formed in Las Vegas, Nevada, all 5 members are from diverse backgrounds including the USA, Switzerland, South Korea, Spain, and Japan.

The Impact

Golden Velvet has tested the boundaries not only with their unique sound, but their large presence as a co-gender and interracial group. The band has maintained a strong place in fan's heart by breaking stereotypes, stigmas, sound barriers, and language barriers.
